Food Duty

Tips for the Food Duty. Hint: It doesn't have to be only pastries :)

Tips and Responsibilities

  • Pastries can be whatever. A lot of us go to Ralphs or Vons and grab sweets. You can do hot food as well. Flip brings breakfast burritos that he makes himself. Lloyd will sometimes bring a quiche. Really up to you!
  • If you are doing pastries it is your responsibility to clean up the table at the end. If you cannot stay until the end you can ask someone else to cover.
